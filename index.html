About the Kingdom

It is a powerful, dynamic and welcoming kingdom. Have no fear for your safety as our forts and numerous soldiers patrol the streets of the whole kingdom. So take advantage of the large market each morning which offers only fresh and regional products but also our great blacksmith who will be happy to make your sword. We welcome heroes from around the world, especially the Argonauts! And the Amazons will be delighted to test your fighting skills around a big fire in the public square.
